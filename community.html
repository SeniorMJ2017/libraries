Connect to the PLIX community

There are many ways to be a part of PLIX. Let's team up! You can...

  • Try out one of our community-designed activities in your library
  • Be a best tester for a new project
  • Apply to participte in our next cohort of Residency Exchanges
  • Join our webinars, meet-up at a conference, and connect with other librarians!

We want to hear from you! Join the PLIX Community.
